our factory

Carpentry factory processing is the process of converting raw lumber into finished products using various types of woodworking machinery. The process typically involves several stages, including planing, cutting, shaping, and joining. At the planing stage, rough lumber is smoothed and leveled using a planer machine.

Next, the lumber is cut into various sizes and shapes using saws or CNC machines. After the cutting stage, the wood is shaped using routers or other shaping tools to achieve the desired design. The joining process involves joining the pieces of wood together using glue, nails, or other fasteners.
